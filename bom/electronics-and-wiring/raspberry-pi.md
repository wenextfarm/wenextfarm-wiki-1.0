**Component tests**{:.internal}

|Test         |Description  |Target       |Tolerance    |
|-------------|-------------|-------------|-------------|
|Model        |Inspect the board for model number.|`Model 4B`|N/A
|Brand        |Inspect the board for the brand name.|`Raspberry Pi`|N/A
|Condition    |Inspect to ensure the product is not used or refurbished.|Product should be brand new|N/A
|GPIO pins    |Inspect the GPIO pins for damage.|No pins should be bent|N/A

> **ℹ️ Some v1.6 kits include different Raspberry Pi models:** Genesis and Genesis XL v1.6 kits shipped before July of 2022 included the Raspberry Pi Model 3B+, kits shipped during or after July of 2022 included the Raspberry Pi Model 4B - 1GB, and kits shipped in January 2023 and later included the Raspberry Pi Model 4B - 2GB.
