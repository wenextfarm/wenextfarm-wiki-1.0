**The type and quantity of accessories may change from time to time. Please refer to the actual items you receive.**

The P1S package comes with a 32 GB Micro SD card (pre-installed in the printer), screen, spool holder, filament, spare hotend, nozzle wiping pads, power cord, unclogging pin tool, PTFE tube, screw pack for scraper, Allen Keys (H1.5 and H2), screw pack for spool holder, screw pack for extruder, hotend clip, Textured PEI Plate (pre-installed on the hotbed), thermal paste, lubricant grease, Quick start guide, disclaimer, and warranty leaflet.

Please note that the products you receive may undergo material changes and version iterations. Therefore, we kindly request you refer to the actual product you receive.
