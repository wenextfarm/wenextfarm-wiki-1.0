## Why We Build WenextFarm?


First of all, we are three passionate young people from China, and we are very passionate about agriculture, and we look forward to the combination of technology and agriculture. We hope to realize more future agricultural through techniques such as automation, vertical cultivation, aquaponics. We hope that through technology, everyone can get green and healthy fresh food easily.
