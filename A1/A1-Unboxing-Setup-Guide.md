1. Unboxing and Removing the Unit
Place the shipping carton horizontally on a flat, level surface.

Cut the cable ties with scissors and open the carton.

https://media/image1.png{width="4.9847222222222225in" height="2.634027777777778in"}

Lift out the foam padding from inside the box.

With two people working together, carefully lift the unit out of the carton and set it down on the foam padding that you have laid out nearby.

https://media/image2.png{width="4.938888888888889in" height="2.5104166666666665in"}

2. Removing the Transit Locking Blocks
Open the accessory tool box from the side and take out the hex key (Allen wrench).

https://media/image3.png{width="4.6819444444444445in" height="2.859027777777778in"}

Remove the blue water tank from the planting container.

https://media/image4.png{width="4.730555555555555in" height="2.888888888888889in"}

Using the hex key, remove all screws marked with red arrows on the blue locking blocks.

IMPORTANT: Be sure to also remove any nuts that may have become wedged inside the aluminum extrusions — if left in place, they can obstruct movement during operation.

https://media/image5.png{width="4.704861111111111in" height="2.4618055555555554in"}

As indicated in the illustration, there are 6 transit locking blocks in total.

https://media/image6.png{width="4.595138888888889in" height="2.7243055555555555in"}

IMPORTANT: One of these blocks has an additional securing screw on its inner side.

https://media/image7.png{width="4.772916666666666in" height="2.86875in"}

All screws that need to be removed are clearly marked with red arrows for your reference.

3. Adjusting the X‑Axis Slide Plates
After removing the locking blocks, switch to the larger hex key and loosen the screws on the X‑axis slide plates by approximately one‑quarter turn on both sides.

IMPORTANT: Do not remove the screws or nuts entirely — simply loosen them.

https://media/image8.png{width="5.0472222222222225in" height="2.79375in"}

4. Installing the Gantry
Take the "M6 screws and nuts" from the accessory box. With the help of an assistant (one person holds the gantry upright and steady while the other inserts the M6 screws through the mounting holes), secure the gantry to the frame.

Note: Screws and nuts should be inserted on both sides, but do not tighten them fully with the hex key just yet.

https://media/image9.png{width="4.963888888888889in" height="2.786111111111111in"}

Alignment steps:

Align the bottom end face of each vertical column extrusion with the notch on the corresponding slide plate;

Ensure the side face of each column sits flush with the inner edge of its slide plate, and that the gantry columns remain perpendicular to the X‑axis rail.

https://media/image10.png{width="5.00625in" height="2.942361111111111in"}

Use the hex key to tighten the two fixing screws on each side.

Repeat the same procedure to secure the opposite side.

5. Installing the Timing Belt Pulleys / Tensioners
Use the hex key to loosen the screw on the timing belt tensioner.

https://media/image11.png{width="4.839583333333334in" height="3.084722222222222in"}

Rotate the tensioner to its horizontal position, place the M4 T‑nut into the slot of the aluminum extrusion, and secure it using the M4 screw.

https://media/image12.png{width="4.518055555555556in" height="2.472916666666667in"}

Turn the tensioner knob to adjust the belt to the appropriate tension.

https://media/image13.png{width="4.624305555555556in" height="2.3868055555555556in"}

Repeat the tensioning procedure for the other two tensioners. (Note: There are 3 tensioners in total — two are located at the ends of the X‑axis extrusion, and one is at the end of the Y‑axis.)

https://media/image14.png{width="4.654166666666667in" height="2.907638888888889in"}

6. Installing the Water Tank and Tool Mount (UTM)
Place the blue water tank onto the support plate of the unit.

https://media/image15.png{width="4.811805555555556in" height="2.470138888888889in"}

Insert the water tube through the hole in the tank lid and down into the tank.

https://media/image16.png{width="4.84375in" height="2.577777777777778in"}

Take the UTM tool head from the box and place it into the UTM bracket as needed.

https://media/image17.png{width="4.822916666666667in" height="2.33125in"}

Note: Ensure the text on the tool head faces outward.

7. Power Connection and Startup
Plug in the power cord. The power indicator light on the control box will turn green, indicating that the unit is receiving power.

https://media/image18.png{width="5.04375in" height="2.845138888888889in"}

Press the power switch — the indicator light will change to red, confirming that the unit is now powered on.

https://media/image19.png{width="4.956944444444445in" height="2.7180555555555554in"}

8. Completion and Next Steps
Congratulations! You have successfully completed the unboxing and basic installation of your WnextFarm A1. You are now ready to proceed with configuration and setup.

Optional accessory: The unit comes with four legs that you can install if desired. Once fitted, the unit can be moved around easily.

https://media/image20.png{width="5.254166666666666in" height="3.7291666666666665in"}

Each of the 4 legs can be rotated to adjust their height (adjustable range: 24–36 cm).
