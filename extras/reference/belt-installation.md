# Step 1: Slide on a belt sleeve
Slide a [belt sleeve](belt sleeve) onto the **belt**.

![belt sleeve on bel](/extras/reference/_images/belt_sleeve_on_bel.png =700x)

# Step 2: Route the belt through the clip
Route the **belt** down through the slot in the [belt clip](belt clip) and then wrap the belt back under itself. The teeth from the short end of the belt should engage with the teeth on the long end.

![belt routed through clip](/extras/reference/_images/belt_routed_through_clip.png =700x)

# Step 3: Secure the belt with the sleeve
Slide the [belt sleeve](belt sleeve) over both ends of the belt. This may take a small bit of force and some wiggling.

![belt secured with sleeve](/extras/reference/_images/belt_secured_with_sleeve.png =700x)

# Step 4: Mount the clip
Mount the clip to the end of an **extrusion** with a [20mm nut bar](20mm nut bar) and [M5 x 10mm screws](M5 x 10mm screws).

![mounted belt clip](/extras/reference/_images/mounted_belt_clip.png =700x)
