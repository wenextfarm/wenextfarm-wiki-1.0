WenextFarm is designed based on FarmBot Genesis, and we are truly grateful to the FarmBot open‑source community for making this project possible. The original CAD models are created in **[Onshape](https://onshape.com)** , giving you full access to view, copy, edit, and export all source 3D CAD models for free from any computer or mobile device. Simply click the links below to access the various assemblies and other resources.

# Copying and modifying parts

Click on ["FarmBot Genesis v1.6"](https://cad.onshape.com/documents/6626b842adca229e69544ad1/v/b5e323b6ae6cf9b43cd65f69/e/3a231755273e980d277c3a4c) to view the CAD models of FarmBot Genesis v1.6. This is because WenextFarm is redesigned and optimized based on FarmBot Genesis v1.6. To make any changes, you need to create your own copy. To do so, you will first need to sign up for a free Onshape account. Then you can click “Make a copy” in the top‑left corner of the Onshape workspace. Onshape will copy the entire workspace into a new document (owned by you), which will include all parts, drawings, assemblies, and other files.

> **Please note**
> 
> When Onshape opens your copied workspace, it may default to a Part Studio named “Screw Hole Templates,” which appears mostly empty. To find and open another Part Studio within the workspace, click the button in the lower‑left corner of the screen to open the Tab Manager. Then browse through the folders or use the search box at the top of the Tab Manager panel to locate the desired Part Studio.
{.is-info}


# Exporting parts

To export a part into another format (such as `.STL` for 3D printing), simply right-click the part and press "Export". Note that you do not need to make a copy of the workspace or have an Onshape account in order to export parts.
