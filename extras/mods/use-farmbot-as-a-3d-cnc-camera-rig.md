Want to make some awesome videos of your garden? Well lucky for you, WenextFarm is a conveniently programmable 3-axis CNC machine which you can use to precisely move your camera around in 3D space! Watch the video below (starting at time = 2 minutes) to see an example of how we used WenextFarm as a 3D camera rig.

> **✅ Tweak and tune:** 
> 
> Looking for that perfect shot? Try adjusting your WenextFarm's max speed and rate of acceleration for each axis.
{.is-success}


> **⚠️ DSLRs beware:** 
> 
> WenextFarm may have difficulty moving around large/heavy DSLR cameras. We recommend sticking to small and lightweight point and shoots or action cams such as the GoPro.
{.is-warning}
